# SORBA

AI Automation Platform for Industrial Intelligence

---

## Overview

SORBA is an AI-driven automation platform focused on improving industrial asset reliability, optimizing operational processes, and enabling sustainable production through end-to-end AutoML.

The platform is designed to remove the dependency on specialized data science teams by providing accessible, no-code machine learning capabilities for real-world industrial environments.

## Key Capabilities

### Operational Intelligence

- Real-time monitoring and anomaly detection
- Root cause analysis for equipment failures
- Continuous learning models that adapt over time

### Process Optimization

- Yield improvement across production lines
- Energy consumption reduction
- Predictive maintenance to prevent downtime

### AI & AutoML

- No-code model creation and training
- Automated feature engineering and model selection
- Continuous retraining based on live data
